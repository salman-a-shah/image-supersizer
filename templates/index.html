About

ImageSupersizer is a free super-resolution service that doubles the resolution of an image while attempting to preserve image quality. This app achieves this through the use of a convolutional residual neural network. This project is currently in alpha stage. Overtime, the supersizing model will be improved and additional models may be added for image enhancement.

This project is created and maintained by Salman Shah. If you'd like to get in touch, feel free to send an email through the contact form.
